The Autism Alliance of Michigan is launching a website called MiAutism in partnership with Detroit Labs that will ultimately allow families to rate and review autism-friendly businesses in the state.

For the first stage of the website, we are collecting initial reviews from the community for Southeast Michigan businesses in 3 categories: restaurants, barbershops/hair salons, and places of recreation (play places, parks, pools, museums, etc.).

Ratings, reviews and locations for favorite places from the autism community.

MiAutism serves the greater Southeast Michigan autistic community by giving recommendations and ratings for restaurants, barber shops, and places to be entertained. Reviews come from parents and the Autisim Alliance of Michigan.

Families in Southeast Michigan can use MiAutism to find restaurants, hair salons, and places of interest by reading reviews and recommendations that come straight from other families with autistic children.
