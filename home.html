Launching in the spring of 2014

MiAutism, a website providing the Southeast Michigan autism community with ratings and recommendations for restaurants, hair salons, and places of recreation.

Add A Review

Use this page to submit reviews of autism-friendly restaurants, hair salons, and places of recreation.

Each submission is then checked by our team to eliminate spam and ensure quality posts.

After your review is checked, it will go live, contributing to a new knowledge base for the autism community!

The Autism Alliance of Michigan and Detroit Labs are partnering to launch MiAutism, a website that will allow families to review and research autism-friendly businesses in the state.

You can use this page to submit reviews for businesses in 3 categories: restaurants, hair salons, and places of recreation.

Submit reviews below and they will be checked for quality, then approved for the site launching in spring 2014.

Ratings, reviews and locations for favorite places from the autism community.

MiAutism will serve Southeast Michigan autistic community by giving recommendations and ratings for restaurants, barber shops, and places to be entertained. Reviews come from parents and the Autism Alliance of Michigan.

Families in Southeast Michigan can use MiAutism to find restaurants, hair salons, and places of interest by reading reviews and recommendations that come straight from other families with autistic children.
